The Layers

25+ components analyzed. A technical breakdown of what "Digital Public Infrastructure" actually means. DPI does not require open source.

NOTE

"India Stack" is PRware, a marketing term coined by iSPIRT. There is no entity called "India Stack." Each system is owned by a different institution: UIDAI (Aadhaar), NPCI (UPI), NHA (ABDM), MeitY (DigiLocker). All closed source. The brand is unified. The code is fragmented and proprietary.

"The idea that every citizen in this country is to be numbered is the primary thing in the project. The second part is that every database should have the number embedded in it so it becomes easy to collate information about people, easy to profile people and launch surveillance if you want."
- Usha Ramanathan
Identity Layer
Aadhaar / UIDAI CLOSED
Governance: Built by private sector brought into government. Volunteer networks promoted ecosystem. Ex-UIDAI members entered Aadhaar markets. RTI-exempt since 2011. "Cronyism" allegations documented.
49% biometric failure rate in Jharkhand. 37% in Rajasthan. No deletion. Fail to match = denied benefits. Not a right, a privilege revocable anytime.
NPR CLOSED
National Population Register. 119 crore residents (Dec 2024). Aadhaar is backend. Linked to NATGRID. Collects 14 demographic fields including biometrics.
No opt-out. No deletion. Feeds surveillance infrastructure. CAA+NPR enables citizenship filtering. "Usual residents" tracked permanently. Aadhaar linkage makes it inescapable.
eKYC CLOSED
Electronic identity verification via Aadhaar. Requires AUA/KUA license from UIDAI. Mandatory for banks, SIMs, financial services.
Fingerprints wear from labor. Corneal blindness, cataracts exclude 2-5% of population. - Usha Ramanathan
Voter ID-Aadhaar Linkage COERCIVE
2021 Election Laws Amendment. Claims "voluntary" but Form 6B offers no real opt-out. In 2015 Telangana pilot, 55 lakh voters removed due to mismatches.
"Biometrics is disenfranchising a large number of citizens." - Usha Ramanathan. SC issued stay on 2015 exercise.
Payments Layer
UPI / NPCI CLOSED
Governance: NPCI claims "private" status but: 51% owned by public sector banks, CAG appoints auditors, Rs 8,730 crore government subsidies (2021-2025). Yet won 2019 CIC case to be RTI-exempt. RBI "failed to ensure public accountability." PhonePe+Google = 86% duopoly (foreign-owned). Parliamentary panel: "foreign entities dominate."
₹805 crore fraud FY26. 1 in 5 users hit by fraud. Only 6% recovered. NPCI sees everything. You see nothing.
OCEN SPEC ONLY
Open Credit Enablement Network. Volunteer network-designed protocol for credit access. Account Aggregator feeds data. Tech philanthropist networks set standards. Claims to "democratize" lending.
Credit decisions based on AA data you cannot verify. Lenders see everything. Rejection reasons opaque. "Open" protocol, closed algorithms. Same governance pattern as DEPA/Beckn.
DBT CLOSED
Direct Benefit Transfer. Aadhaar-linked welfare distribution. 1200+ schemes. Claims "savings" from reduced leakage.
"Savings" = denial to excluded. 88% of cancelled cards were genuine. (J-PAL)
PFMS CLOSED
Public Financial Management System. "Master control software of government finances." Single Nodal Agency model (2021) stripped state financial autonomy.
"Code is Law", PFMS enables unconstitutional centralization through tech. States reduced to "data nodes." Federalism crushed.
Data Layer
Account Aggregator (DEPA) SPEC ONLY
Governance: Sahamati (SRO) launched by tech philanthropist network. Volunteer networks designed DEPA. Industry self-regulates on pricing, privacy, encryption. Consumer protection secondary to "participatory governance."
Single consent for everything. SBI MD: "Most customers don't read fine print." No way to verify deletion. Consent under exclusion threat isn't consent.
DigiLocker CLAIMS OPEN
Governance: NIC-operated. 2020 critical vulnerability allowed OTP bypass. SIM swap vulnerability documented. Fake apps proliferating. Cannot self-host or federate.
SIM recycling exposes previous owner's Aadhaar (2025 case). Fake apps stealing data (Dec 2025 govt warning). Cannot delink Aadhaar once linked. No self-hosting.
API Setu GATEKEPT
Open API Platform (MeitY, 2020). 4200+ APIs, 1800+ partners. Enables interoperability but centralizes access control.
Single gateway. All data flows through one chokepoint. Private providers need approval. The node that controls access controls everything.
Digi Yatra FRT
Governance: Digi Yatra Foundation = private company (AAI 26% + 5 private airports). RTI-exempt. 29% enrolled without knowledge. Forces Aadhaar. DPDP Act Section 17 can exempt it anytime.
30% enrolled without knowing. IFF: "tool of coercion." Face data enables dragnet surveillance. Private foundation, not ministry. Cannot verify deletion claims.
Commerce Layer
ONDC NOT FOSS
Governance: Beckn protocol by tech philanthropist networks. Beckn Foundation owned by single philanthropist. ONDC Advisory Council includes same network. "Open" but not FOSS. Subsidy-dependent, collapsed 30% when cut.
Subsidy-dependent. Orders collapsed 30% when subsidies cut. Not FOSS. No community governance. "Open" in name only. Inc42
GeM CLOSED
Government e-Marketplace. ₹13.6 lakh crore GMV. 23 lakh sellers. Mandatory for government procurement.
Mandatory gateway. No GeM = no government contracts. Single chokepoint controls all procurement. Payment delays. Excludes vendors without digital capacity.
Beckn Protocol SPEC ONLY
Governance: FIDE (Foundation for Interoperability in Digital Economy) sets standards, co-founded by tech philanthropist network. Pattern mirrors ReBIT→Sahamati. Specs "open," governance concentrated. Google Cloud partnership 2025.
Protocol is open. Implementations are closed. Google Cloud partnership (2025). 63M businesses must adopt vs 50 banks for UPI. Open spec, closed reality.
Health Layer
ABDM / ABHA CLOSED
Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission. 80 crore ABHA IDs. 67 crore health records linked. Claims "Privacy by Design." Samsung integration expands attack surface.
PMJAY dashboard exposes patient details (MediaNama 2024). Star Health breach: 31M records on Telegram. No separate law, legally challengeable. MediaNama
CoWIN / U-WIN CLOSED
Vaccination registration. 1.6B doses recorded. Portal unavailable since Aug 2025. Being repurposed as U-WIN for universal immunization.
200-280M Indians had no/poor internet access. CoWIN data breach affected hundreds of millions. Digital exclusion by design.
Aarogya Setu CLOSED
Contact tracing app. 218M downloads. No legislative backing. Privacy policy contradicts protocol on data sharing.
Karnataka HC: "Cannot deny benefit for not installing." MIT rating: 1/5 for transparency. GPS tracking without disclosure.
UHID CLOSED
Unique Health ID. Hospital-level patient tracking. AIIMS-originated. Aadhaar-linkable. Enables lifelong medical profile across all healthcare facilities.
Permanent patient tracking. Aadhaar linkage enables cross-database profiling. UHID Rules 2021 allow collection of sexual orientation, religious/political beliefs. TB patients forced to link, some dropped treatment fearing privacy breach.
eSanjeevani CLAIMS SECURE
National Telemedicine Service. 311M+ patients served. Worlds largest government-led telemedicine. Claims EHR compliance.
311M patients. No independent audit. Code closed. "Secure" is a claim you cannot verify. Integrates with ABDM, your consultation becomes lifelong record.
Education Layer
DIKSHA CLOSED
Governance: NCERT/MoE platform. Made mandatory without alternative. Ministry ignored breach alerts. No accountability. HRW documented location tracking + advertising trackers in children's app.
Major breach: 1M+ teachers, 600K students exposed. Data searchable on Google for 4+ years. HRW: "violated children's privacy." Location tracking without disclosure. Advertising trackers found. HRW
APAAR NO LAW
Governance: No legal/statutory backing, only ministry circulars. "Voluntary-mandatory" design like Aadhaar. School principals threatened with salary cuts. IFF: open APIs expose children's data. Tracks from age 6 for life.
Children tracked from age 6 forever. No delete button. No opt-out. Parents pressured during admissions. The registry never forgets.
Governance Layer
eOffice → Zoho PRIVATIZED
12 lakh govt emails migrated to Zoho (Oct 2025). 7-year contract awarded 2023. PMO included. NIC's 50-year capability abandoned.
"Digital sovereignty surrendered." Zoho founder on National Security Advisory Board since 2021. Cabinet notes, diplomatic cables now depend on private contract. No end-to-end encryption by default.
UMANG UNVERIFIED
Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance. MeitY (2017). Single app for 1200+ government services. Aadhaar-based authentication.
1200+ services through one app. Claims "no data collection", Apple: "not verified." Single aggregation point sees all citizen-government interaction.
PM Gati Shakti DATA SHARING
Infrastructure planning portal. GIS-based. Land records, ports, forests, highways data. Feb 2025: Govt to share data with private sector.
Ports, railways, energy data on one platform. Now sharing with private sector. Single visibility point for all national infrastructure. Hub-spoke by design.
Justice Layer
eCourts / NJDG CLOSED
National Judicial Data Grid. 32+ crore judgments. Judges govern themselves. Vision drafted by think tanks funded by philanthropic networks. No parliamentary oversight. No RTI.
Judiciary exempt from data protection. No Right to be Forgotten, SC blocked attempts. POCSO victim names searchable. Divorce details permanent. Acquitted? Still marked forever. Selective digitization, some vanish, yours persists. The institution that interprets your rights exempts itself from them.
Surveillance Layer
NATGRID CLOSED
Governance: RTI-exempt since 2011. Now linked to NPR (119 crore residents, Dec 2024). 100 crore facial entries. 45,000+ queries/month. Access expanded to SP-rank officers. No judicial oversight.
RTI-exempt since 2011. No judicial oversight. "Aadhaar with NATGRID is surveillance." (CIS India)
CMS CLOSED
Governance: C-DOT built, TERM cells operate. Direct access bypasses telecom providers. 2024 Interception Rules lowered threshold, "consider" alternatives, not "determine." Delhi HC ordered halt (pending). No judicial oversight.
Mass telecom interception. No judicial oversight. No transparency. No accountability. No legal framework. The panopticon has no windows.
